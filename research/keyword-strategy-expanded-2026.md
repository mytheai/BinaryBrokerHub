### 6.4 Yandex SEO Notes

1. **Keyword "отзывы" (reviews/feedback) is the #1 modifier** -- Russian users search "[brand] отзывы" far more than "[brand] review". Ensure RU pages use "отзывы" in title tags, H1, and meta descriptions.

2. **"Развод" (divorce/scam) and "скам" (scam)** are the trust-query modifiers. Russian users search "quotex развод" or "pocket option скам или нет" -- equivalent to English "is X a scam".

3. **"Вывод денег/средств" (withdrawal)** is the most common action keyword in Russian. Payment-related content performs extremely well on Yandex.

4. **Yandex Webmaster Tools** -- register the site separately from Google Search Console. Submit sitemap.xml and set geo-targeting to Russia/CIS.

5. **Yandex IKS (Site Quality Index)** -- replaces old TIC. Focus on behavioral metrics: dwell time > 2 min, low bounce rate, deep scroll on RU pages.

6. **Turbo Pages** -- Yandex's AMP equivalent. Consider implementing for mobile RU users.

7. **Yandex.Zen** (now "Dzen") -- publishing platform. Create a Dzen channel for RU content syndication to build Yandex authority.

## 7. BING-SPECIFIC CONSIDERATIONS

Bing has ~3-8% global market share but is significant in specific demographics (older users, corporate, Edge browser default). Key differences:

1. **Bing rewards exact-match domains and keywords more than Google** -- ensure title tags contain exact keywords
2. **Bing Webmaster Tools** -- submit sitemap separately, claim URL
3. **Bing Places** -- not applicable for an affiliate site
4. **Social signals matter more on Bing** -- Reddit, Twitter, and social shares influence Bing rankings more than Google
5. **Bing favors older, established content** -- update dates matter less; focus on comprehensive depth
6. **Bing AI / Copilot** -- structured data (FAQ schema, Review schema) increases chances of being cited in Bing Chat/Copilot answers
