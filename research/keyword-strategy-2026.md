## KEY INSIGHT: WHY THIS STRATEGY WORKS FAST

1. **Brand keywords have lower competition** than generic terms. "Quotex review" is easier to rank for than "binary options trading".

2. **Deep, comprehensive content beats thin content.** Your 7,000+ word review pages will outrank 500-word summaries on general sites.

3. **Multi-language = 3x the keyword opportunity** with the same content translated. RU and ES markets have LESS English competition.

4. **"Is X real or fake" queries** are the FASTEST to rank for because they're long-tail, high-intent, and most sites don't create dedicated pages for them.

5. **Promo code pages** are evergreen traffic magnets that people search for every month (note: "quotex promo code 2026" already in autocomplete).

6. **Geo-specific content** (India, Pakistan, Nigeria, Colombia) has almost ZERO competition from English-language specialists.
