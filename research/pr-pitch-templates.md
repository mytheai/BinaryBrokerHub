## Rules of engagement (the part that actually wins links)

- **Speed > polish.** Reply same day; journalists work on deadline.
- **Relevance filter.** Only pitch queries about scams, consumer protection, regulation, fintech risk.
  Skip "trading tips" requests — wrong angle, and platforms penalise spray-and-pray.
- **One link, in context.** The tracker as a data source. Never paste affiliate links.
- **Always disclose.** "We're an affiliate, and here's the independent data" is the whole pitch.
- **Track it.** Log every pitch (date, outlet, query, outcome) so you double down on what lands.
- **Volume.** Expect a low hit rate — a handful of placements from dozens of pitches is a win, and each
  is a high-authority backlink + brand mention.
